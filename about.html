What is SquadConnect?

SquadConnect is a website where your sports team can work together and stay connected. You can use it to organize your games or just keep in touch with your team.

Group Chats

Create and join group chats to communicate with your team members. Share ideas, send out the dates to the games and stay connected wherever you are.

Team Calendar

Each group chat has its own calendar where the group leader can schedule games and events. Access the calendar directly from your chat interface.

Easy to Use

Our good simple design makes it easy for teams of any size to get in contact. Their is no complicated setup required.

Get Started Today

Ready to improve your team's collaboration? Head over to our Group Chats page to create or join a group. Once you're in a chat, use the team calendar to schedule your games and events!
